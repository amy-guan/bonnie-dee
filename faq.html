1. What does Bonnie do?
Bonnie answers your questions, providing you with information about mental, sexual, or general health issues.
2. Why was Bonnie created?
We've found that many young women and girls have trouble accessing reliable, good information about certain topics as they grow older, such as periods or pregnancy. We created Bonnie in the hopes that she'll provide a safe and inconspicuous way for individuals to learn more about themselves.
3. Why the name Bonnie Dee?
Bonnie Dee refers to Bona Dea, the Roman goddess of fertility in women, healing, and protection. We hope Bonnie will be able to help people as much as Bona Dea has!
4. Who created Bonnie?
Bonnie Dee was created at ByteHacks in NYC at Spotify HQ by Cindy Zhu, Amy Guan, and Claire Kim.
